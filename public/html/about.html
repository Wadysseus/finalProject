Hi, thanks for clicking on the ABOUT page.

This is my final project for RefactorU, I hope you enjoy it.

One guideline we were given to help us come up with ideas for apps was to try to think of a problem in our own lives we wanted solved.

I don't really have any big problems to solve, but I do go through a lot of ink and paper for role-playing games. See, I used to live in a big house full of gamers, and there'd always be character sheets on hand. We'd print out extras, because we knew they'd get used.

These days, everyone's scattered to the wind, and even if we do meet up and make characters, we usually end up playing for only a handful of sessions before life gets in the way. This means that there's a cabinet in my house that's basically a repository for aborted campaigns and characters.

Now, I figure, rather than continue to spend inordinate amounts of cash on the most expensive liquid on Earth, I'd save the planet and my pocketbook by creating a site that allows for easy digital record keeping for characters. There are a lot of good character builder sites out there, but not so many that allow you to retain a living document, something to replace the old "scratch sheet."
